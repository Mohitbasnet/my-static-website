Studying a UK Degree from Kathmandu

Pursuing a UK degree in Nepal provides access to international curricula and assessment standards without the requirement to relocate. At ISMT, programmes are delivered in accordance with partner university regulations so that learning outcomes, assessment methods, and academic expectations are consistent.

Students are taught by qualified faculty and study in comparatively small groups, which facilitates interaction, timely feedback, and ongoing academic support. This environment enables students to engage with the material in depth and to develop both subject knowledge and essential study skills.

In addition to formal teaching, students may participate in co-curricular activities such as clubs, events, and workshops. These activities are intended to complement academic study and to support the development of professional and interpersonal competencies that are relevant to further study and employment.
